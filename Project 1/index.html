Aparna Vastarey

1966-2024

Aparna Vastarey was an Indian actress, television presenter, famous anchor of Karnataka and Radio jockey. A popular face in kannada telivision, she was known for her work as a presenter of various shows that aired on DD Chandana. She has hosted more than 8000 shows and also she is the voice behind Namma Metro Bengaluru
She passed away on 11 july 2024 after a two-year battle with lung cancer. It is the great loss to our Karnataka.
